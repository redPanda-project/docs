== Architectural Decisions

=== ADR-01: Stateless Full Nodes
*   **Decision**: Full Nodes shall not maintain per-connection state for routing.
*   **Rationale**: Enables mobile clients to switch networks (WiFi/4G) without breaking sessions. Reduces memory burden on nodes.
*   **Consequence**: We must use "Packet Switching" (Flaschenpost) and Self-Contained Tokens (RGB).

=== ADR-02: Reverse Garlic for Retrieval
*   **Decision**: Clients must not perform direct `GET` operations on the DHT for their own messages.
*   **Rationale**: Direct lookups leak metadata ("Who is looking for what").
*   **Consequence**: Increased latency (Request + Reply path) compared to direct fetch, but necessary for privacy.

=== ADR-03: Two-Layer ACK
*   **Decision**: Separate Network-level ACKs from User-level ACKs.
*   **Rationale**: Mobile clients are often offline. Penalizing nodes for offline clients creates bad incentives.
