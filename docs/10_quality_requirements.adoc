== Quality Requirements

=== Quality Tree

*   **Privacy**
    ** **Unlinkability**: Attackers cannot correlate Sender and Receiver.
    ** **Deniability**: Messages should not provide cryptographic proof of authorship to third parties (where possible).
*   **Reliability**
    ** **Offline Delivery**: Messages must arrive even if the recipient is offline for 24h.
    ** **Partition Tolerance**: The network must heal around broken nodes.
*   **Efficiency**
    ** **Battery Life**: The protocol must minimize radio wake-ups.
    ** **Data cap**: Overhead (Garlic headers) must be kept reasonable.

=== Scenarios

*   **Scenario 1 (Subway)**: Alice enters a subway, loses web. Her messages are buffered at her Outbound Handle. When she exits 20 mins later, Push wakes the app, and messages are delivered.
*   **Scenario 2 (Node Failure)**: The node `H2` in a path fails. The packet is dropped. The lack of R-ACK triggers Alice to re-send via a new path.
